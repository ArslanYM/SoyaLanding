About

Shivam Soya is the commodities trading arm of Shivam Group. The journey of Shivam Group as it is presently known started as a trading concern with the firm, 'Krishan Chand Mukesh Kumar' getting registered in the city of Karnal, Haryana in the early nineties. The foundation of the said firm was laid by Shree Krishan Chand, father of the current Managing Director of Shivam Soya, Mr. Mukesh Kumar Goyal. For the purpose of enhanced business expansion opportunities, the next associate company was registered as 'Shivam Soya Pvt. Ltd.' in 2010, with its headquarters in the city of Kota (Rajasthan).
